chosen

option_id
O2
rationale
O2 minimizes duplication (mitigates drift risk implicit in Investigate r1/r2 — reconciliation must remain visible at the conflict site, not buried in a new long-form file). It respects AGENTS.md policy c2 ("prompts over finished products"). The reconciliation appendix lives next to the literal AGENTS.md:7/9/10 lines that Investigate flagged as ambiguous, so any future reader sees both the original claim and the agreed disambiguation. O1 was rejected for duplication; O3 was rejected because mixing workflow narrative into AGENTS.md (which doubles as a rules ledger) increases edit-conflict risk.

  3. Append a short "Reconciliation note" subsection to AGENTS.md that points to docs/workflow.md and records the D-A and D-B decisions verbatim.
  4. Verify cross-links resolve and per-step README anchors exist.

policies

  • Language policy (inherited c1): documentation files (docs/workflow.md, AGENTS.md edit) written in English.
  • No content duplication (inherited c2): docs/workflow.md must not paste per-step README bodies; it links to them.
  • Reconciliation visibility: every numeric or enumerative claim that disagrees with raw filesystem evidence (9 vs 10 steps; 16-agent split) must be accompanied by an inline reconciliation pointer to D-A or D-B.
  • Tool-association caveat (inherited Investigate r3): pyreez/firebat/emberdeck step associations rendered with a "declared in AGENTS.md; runtime presence not verified at git_HEAD 4e74abd3" footnote.
  • HEAD pinning: docs/workflow.md records the source git_HEAD against which enumerations were derived.
  • Triage placement: Triage documented as a pre-flow gate under a separate heading, not under the Flow step pool.

user_flows

  1. New contributor onboarding: lands on README/AGENTS.md → sees reconciliation pointer → opens docs/workflow.md → reads ordering diagram + glossary → drills into steps/<step>/README.md for a specific step prompt.
  2. Agent author cross-check: when adding or modifying an agent under .claude/agents/, consult docs/workflow.md agent-role table to confirm producer/reviewer suffix convention (D-B).
  3. Triage authoring: triage author follows the "Pre-flow gate" section in docs/workflow.md, then consults steps/triage/README.md plus its two sub-files (compound-recursion.md, failure-routing.md).
